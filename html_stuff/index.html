What we do

Founded in 2012, Cloud Runners Ltd was established in Lancaster, UK, to provide infrastructure design and management services to companies, who, wish to make the most of the flexibility, scalability and automation that modern cloud hosting can provide, but would also like support and help to get them there.

More about Cloud Runners

Our philosophy is that we like our clients to consider us as an extension to their team. We take our clients' projects on as though they were our own and we work with you to choose the right cloud services for your applications and your budget.

Modern web applications are increasingly needing global connectivity and often must work on a variety of mobile devices and this is where the power of large scale cloud infrastructure such as Amazon EC2 comes into it's own, but ... still, for many projects, traditional set-ups are still appropriate. We are here to assist you and your applications, and to be there when you need support that you can rely upon. Cloud Runners are actively looking for additional clients, so please get in touch.
