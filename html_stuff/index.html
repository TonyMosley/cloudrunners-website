Cloud Infrastructure

Design, Build, Support

Cloud Runners can help you build or move your applications and services to the cloud and manage and support you in production.

Dependable > Experienced > Business foucessed

Founded in 2012 and incorporated in early 2013 we are a technology start-up based out of historic Lancaster in the North West of England. We provide quality, professional, technology led web and mobile solutions built around purposed infrastructure and bespoke software development. We will work with your team and your business goals to design, build, and support the infrastructure and applications that drive your business.

Our core business is the design and support of application infrastructure using infrastructure as a service providers (IaaS) (for example, Amazon AWS, Rackspace Cloud, Rightscale) and platform as a service (Paas) (for example CloudBees, EngineYard). We are also equipped to take on full application development for web, mobile web and mobile applications.

We are business focussed, approachable techies; we will work with you to get results and support you through the full lifecycle of your applications and services. If you are have a new project about to start or want to migrate existing services to cloud hosting, please get in touch.

  • Design and build.

    We can design and build systems infrastructure alongside your application and the whole stack if required.

  • Monitor your infrastructure

    Using operating system level tools and metrics to help keep your infrastructure healthy and available.

  • Monitor your application

    Employing the latest and greatest application performance monitoring tools to make sure your systems are responsive and your end users are happy.

  • Migrate your infrastructure or application to the cloud

    moving your system to the cloud enabling you to save money and time.

  • Back up your system

    Helping you maintain a solid backup strategy that complies with your particular requirements as well as helping you ensure that your system is available and, should the worst happen, recoverable.

  • Automate your test and deployment

    Helping you find ways to automate your test and deployment procedures to create stable systems that can be replicated in multiple environments for development, testing, staging and production.
